f the Constitution sets out the definition and terms of the Executive Branch of Government for the United States of America. The executive branch of any governmental structure is the one most likely to become powerful, thanks to the nature of executive power. In most countries, when a dictatorship or despotic form of government forms, the despot comes from what was once the executive branch of the government. Indeed, in the British system of the time, a great deal of power was invested in the monarch of Britain, thus empowering the executive branch of that government,The Second Article of the Constitution, then, was an attempt to help define executive powers without letting them overrun the Government. It was aimed at both establishing the nature and election procedures for the executive branch, while also ensuring that there were some limits to the powers of that executive branch.





Flexbox is a CSS3 design module that was created to improve the way in which responsive design is done, thus avoiding the use of float, writing less code and facilitating the positioning of elements, even having no notion of their size. Basically the idea of ​​Flexbox is to be able to alter the width, height and positioning of elements in the best way with the space we have.The Flexbox structure is made up of parent and child containers (Container-Flex and Elements-Flex respectively). If we want to use the properties of Flexbox we will have to define it through the display property and its flex or inline-flex value within the selector that we define as our parent element or Container-Flex. That is why Flexbox gives us endless possibilities to create layouts for the components of our web pages, in a simple way and very easy to learn.
